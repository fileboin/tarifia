## Tarifia: Open Source payments infrastructure for the 21st century

Focus on building your passion, while we focus on the infrastructure to get you paid.

- Sell SaaS and digital products in minutes
- All-in-one funding & monetization platform for developers.
- Sell access to GitHub repositories, Discord Support channels, File Downloads, License Keys & much more with Digital Products & Subscriptions.
- We're the merchant of record handling the...
    - ...boilerplate (billing, receipts, customer accounts etc)
    - ...headaches (sales tax, VAT)

## Pricing

- Starts at 5% + 50¢ (Starter plan, free)
- Optional paid plans (Pro, Growth, Scale) with lower rates
- Additional fees may apply. [Read more](https://tarifia.sh/docs/documentation/tarifia-as-merchant-of-record/fees)

## Roadmap, Issues & Feature Requests

**💬 Feature requests & bugs** can be reported through the "Feedback" button found in the sidebar, bottom left of your Tarifia dashboard.

**🔓 Found a security vulnerability?** We greatly appreciate responsible and private disclosures. See [Security](./SECURITY.md)

### Tarifia API & SDK

You can integrate Tarifia on your docs, sites or services using our [Public API](https://tarifia.sh/docs/api-reference) and [Webhook API](https://tarifia.sh/docs/integrate/webhooks/endpoints).

We also maintain SDKs for the following languages:

- JavaScript (Node.js and browsers): [tarifiasource/tarifia-js](https://github.com/tarifiasource/tarifia-js)
- Python: [tarifiasource/tarifia-python](https://github.com/tarifiasource/tarifia-python)
