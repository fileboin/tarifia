To support Tarifia in delivering its services, Tarifia engages third-party service providers and affiliates to support the delivery and operation of the Tarifia platform.

**What is a Sub-processor?**
Tarifia’s third-party service providers and affiliates, referred to as **Sub-processors** under the General Data Protection Regulation (“GDPR”), may process personal data on behalf of Tarifia and its merchants.

Sub-processors are service providers that may access or process personal data on behalf of Tarifia in order to provide infrastructure, operational support, or other services necessary for Tarifia to deliver its platform.

This page provides information about the Sub-processors and other Service Providers Tarifia uses, including their locations and a description of the services they perform.

**Due Diligence**

We perform due diligence including a security assessment on all of our service providers, including sub-processors. We also ensure that our contractual terms require that they process personal data only for the purposes of providing services to Tarifia and in accordance with applicable data protection laws and our terms.
